HUONG DAN CHAY GAME CO CARO (GOMOKU)
=====================================

YEU CAU:
  Windows 10/11 (64-bit). Khong can cai dat them gi.

CHAY GAME:
  Bam doi click vao "CaroGame.exe"

PHIM TAT:
  Mouse trai      Dat quan tai o tro
  W / A / S / D  Di chuyen con tro (keyboard)
  Enter           Dat quan tai vi tri con tro
  ESC             Mo / dong Pause menu
  R               Van moi (giu diem)
  L               Luu van dau
  T               Tai van da luu

LUAT THANG:
  Noi duoc 5 quan lien tiep (ngang / doc / cheo).
  Ban co 12x12.

CHE DO:
  PVP  - Hai nguoi tren cung mot may
  PVE  - Nguoi vs AI (De / Trung binh / Kho)

GHI CHU:
  Thu muc "Saves/" chua file luu game (tu dong tao khi chay lan dau).
  Thu muc "Log/"   chua benchmark log cua AI.
